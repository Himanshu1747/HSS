STOR-X® Products | Materials & Warranty Information

High Quality Materials & Professional Installation

STOR-X® Organizing Systems have a one year Warranty on materials. This warranty covers all STOR-X® Products – Shelving, Storage, Components, and Drawers. For questions or concerns regarding the STOR-X® warranty, you may contact Home Storage Solutions at (360) 670-3297. You can also email us at HSS@olypen.com. Our installation and workmanship is covered for as long at the original purchaser owns their home; less issues deemed abuse by Home Storage Solutions, LLC.

STOR-X® Organizing Systems are custom built, modular, and wood laminate-based storage systems that use European cabinet technology to achieve a highly flexible design in numerous applications. We offer over 15 environmentally friendly finish options. We also offer tie and belt racks, garment valets and roll-out baskets as well as many more accessory options to personalize your custom storage solutions.

RaceDeck®

Turn Your Garage Into A Showroom

RaceDeck® floors have been featured on This Old House, Extreme Makeover: Home Edition, SPEED, ESPN 2, The Discovery Channel, My Classic Car and many others. When people want a professional, clean and cool custom garage floor, they turn to Home Storage Solutions and request RaceDeck®.

The product is installed as flooring squares, making it super easy to replace damage that may occur over time.
