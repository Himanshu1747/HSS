Proudly Serving The Greater Puget Sound Area, from Blaine at the Canadian Border to Olympia and all of the Olympic Peninsula.
My name is John Plake and I live in Port Orchard, Washington. I would like to introduce you to my business, Home Storage Solutions. I started the business over 11 years ago to serve the Olympic Peninsula, providing solutions to easy and difficult storage and closet problems.

Custom Designed Closets

Professionally Designed & Expertly Installed
The great thing of about our custom closets is the fact that our customers get to pick the exact cabinetry and shelving configuration they want. Using the modular approach, they are able to have storage compartments placed and configured where they want them. A custom closet allows you to have your clothes properly hung to avoid wrinkles, your shoes up off the ground to avoid dust and cabinets for your essentials right where you need them. Looking to finally create that perfect custom closet? The team at Home Storage Solutions can make that happen for you!

Custom Designed Kitchen Pantries

Built With The Highest Quality Materials
Tired of dreaming about the perfect kitchen pantry? There is simply no time like the present to make that dream become a reality. The Leader In Custom Storage Solutions

Thinking of Getting Organized? There Is Simply No Time Like The Present. Let Our Team Here At Home Storage Solutions Show You How To Get Started!
Whether it’s your home office, a dream garage workshop, a custom kitchen pantry, storage for your laundry room, walk-in closet, or kids play room, Home Storage Solutions will make it happen. Our firm has over 30 years of experience and can handle any size custom storage job. We are fully licensed, bonded and insured and offer custom storage for both residential and commercial applications.
Although we are located on the Olympic Peninsula, we do serve the entire Puget Sound area from Blaine to Olympia. We serve customers in Jefferson county, Clallam county and Kitsap county. With over 30 years of custom furniture construction experience, we are able to professionally design the absolute perfect storage solutions.
